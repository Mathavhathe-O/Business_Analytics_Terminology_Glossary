# Business_Analytics_Terminology_Glossary
This repository contains a research-based glossary of key concepts, metrics, and KPIs used in Business Analytics. The project was completed as part of a Business Analytics Foundations course and focuses on building a strong understanding of the language used in data-driven decision-making.

The goal of this assignment was to define and apply 38 essential business analytics terms across four key areas:

- Foundational Concepts of Analytics  
- Data, Measurement & Reporting  
- Customer & Sales KPIs  
- Marketing & Campaign Analytics  

Each term includes:
- A clear, original definition (in my own words)
- A real-world business example

## 🧠 Key Skills Demonstrated

- Data literacy and conceptual understanding  
- Ability to distinguish between similar analytics concepts (e.g. KPI vs Metric, CAC vs CLV)  
- Understanding of core business and marketing KPIs  
- Translating technical concepts into simple, practical explanations  
- Research and academic referencing  

## 📊 Why This Project Matters

Understanding these concepts is essential for any aspiring data analyst. This glossary forms the foundation for:
- Data-driven decision making  
- Business performance analysis  
- Marketing and customer analytics  
- Real-world data projects  

## 🛠 Tools & Concepts Covered

- Business Analytics & Data Analytics  
- Business Intelligence (BI)  
- Data Visualization & Dashboards  
- KPIs such as Conversion Rate, CAC, CLV, NPS  
- Marketing metrics like CTR, ROI, ROAS, and Attribution
